# Business Case Study

## Project Contents
- `Python_Data_Analytics.ipynb`: Main analysis notebook with data exploration and insights.
- `Data_Analytics_Report.pdf`: Written report summarizing findings.
- `Business_Case_Study.pptx`: Presentation-ready slide deck.
- `Assignment.txt`: Case study prompt and recommended analysis questions.

## Case Study Questions
- How many customers joined the company during the last quarter? How many customers joined overall?
- What is the customer profile for customers that churned, joined, and stayed? Are they different?
- What seem to be the key drivers of customer churn?
- Is the company losing high-value customers? If so, how can they retain them?

## How to Use
1. Open `Python_Data_Analytics.ipynb` in Jupyter Notebook or JupyterLab.
2. Review `Data_Analytics_Report.pdf` for a narrative summary.
3. Use `Business_Case_Study.pptx` for presentation or stakeholder review.

## Notes
The dataset referenced in the case study is not included in this repository. Ensure you have access to the customer churn dataset before running the notebook.
